About Me

Commenced video editing passion at age 12, "starting with Windows Movie Maker before advancing to Sony Vegas. Transitioned to Premiere Pro during later teens. Proficient in crafting music videos and documentaries for personal music projects, along with collaborating on fellow artists' music videos. Well-versed in After Effects, adept at creating compelling motion graphics, typography, and cinematic effects. Experienced in multicam podcast editing and talk show production."

"Embedded in the core of the music industry, I'm a Sound Mixing and Mastering Engineer driven by an enduring passion for sonic excellence. My musical journey, from early ensemble collaborations in school bands to a vibrant career as a rap artist, laid the foundation for my expertise. Beyond a mere profession, it's a heartfelt commitment. Behind the scenes, I meticulously refine and sculpt sound, focusing on the nuanced art of mixing and mastering. I specialize in translating artists' creative visions into polished, resonant productions, ensuring each project embodies their distinctive artistry."
